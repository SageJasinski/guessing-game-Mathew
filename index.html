Biography

I am in the Army National Guard and that has allowed me to travel to some interesting palces. I am married with 2 kids and a cat. My wife and I have been married going on 16 years and we have a 9 year old son and 5 year old daughter. Our cat is a covid cat and he is almost 2 years old.

Education

I graduated from Arizona State University with a degree in Interdisciplinary Studies. My concentrations were business and music. I also have a minor in Military leadership. I have also attended Communication Strategy course through the Department of Defense, which qualified me as a Public Affairs Officer for the Army. I have also attended a Signal Officer course where I earned a COMPTIA Security + certification. In addition, I have a real estate license, Life, Property and Casualty insurance licenses and a secret security clearance.

Experience

Prior to real estate I spent a big portion of my career working in insurance, helping brokers find solutions in the employee benefit space. I focused on life, dental, vision, disability insurance and ancillary worksite benefits.
